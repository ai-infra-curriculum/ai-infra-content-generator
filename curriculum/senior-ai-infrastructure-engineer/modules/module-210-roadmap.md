# Module Roadmap

## Module Overview

- **Module ID**: MOD-210
- **Module Title**: Technical Leadership & Mentorship
- **Target Role(s)**: Senior AI Infrastructure Engineer
- **Duration**: 30 hours (10 lecture, 12 lab, 6 project, 2 assessment)
- **Prerequisites**: MOD-206
- **Next Module(s)**: PROJ-304, cross-role leadership initiatives

## Cross-Role Progression

- **Builds On**: Platform engineering outcomes from MOD-206.
- **Adds New Depth**: Strategic planning, communication, mentorship, stakeholder alignment.
- **Shared Assets**: Outputs referenced by program leadership, applied to PROJ-304 retrospectives.
- **Differentiators**: Focus on people leadership, executive communication, and architectural decision stewardship.

## Learning Objectives

- Facilitate design reviews, author ADRs, and drive cross-functional decision-making.
- Mentor engineers, deliver feedback, and build learning programs for AI infrastructure teams.
- Communicate technical strategy to executives and stakeholders through compelling narratives.

1. Leadership models for infrastructure teams (servant leadership, situational leadership).

3. Communication artifacts (ADRs, executive briefs, public updates, documentation standards).
4. Mentorship frameworks (skill matrices, growth plans, feedback loops, performance coaching).
5. Stakeholder management (product/security alignment, negotiation, conflict resolution).
6. Knowledge sharing (tech talks, internal blogs, open source/community engagement).
