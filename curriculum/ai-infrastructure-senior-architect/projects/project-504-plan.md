## Project Narrative

The organization needs a dedicated AI innovation lab to accelerate emerging technology adoption. You will:

1. Define vision, scope, and success criteria for the innovation program.
2. Build operating model (intake, funding, staffing, IP management) and governance.
3. Select and negotiate partnerships (hyperscalers, startups, academia) with co-innovation frameworks.
4. Develop KPI dashboard and reporting structure for executives.
5. Present investment proposal and pilot roadmap to innovation steering committee.

## Deliverables

- Innovation program charter, operating model, RACI, funding plan.
- Technology radar, intake workflow, evaluation criteria, backlog.
- Partnership strategy, governance templates, joint roadmap.
- KPI dashboard (innovation velocity, ROI, adoption, IP metrics).
- Executive investment brief and communication plan.

## Constraints & Assumptions

- Must align with corporate innovation strategy, legal/IP policies, and budget guardrails.
- Include sustainability, responsible AI, and compliance considerations for pilots.
- Provide pilot roadmap with success metrics and gating criteria.
- Engage finance, legal, procurement, and HR stakeholders for approvals.
