## Project Narrative

The organization plans a $150M AI infrastructure transformation: consolidating platforms, expanding globally, and enabling responsible AI. You will:

1. Assess current capabilities, maturity, and business priorities across divisions.
2. Define target-state architecture, operating model, and investment roadmap.
3. Quantify costs, ROI, risk, and sustainability metrics; align with corporate strategy.
4. Present strategy to executive steering committee and board finance committee.
5. Produce change management plan, talent strategy, and measurement framework.

## Deliverables

- Strategy dossier (vision, capability map, roadmap, KPI dashboard, risk register).
- Financial model (TCO, ROI, sensitivity analysis, sustainability metrics).
- Organizational change & talent plan (org design, enablement, communications).
- Executive presentation and investment recommendation memo.
- Integration/merger evaluation (if applicable) with due diligence summary.

## Constraints & Assumptions

- Strategy must align with regulatory/sustainability targets and existing transformation programs.
- Budget envelope defined; recommend phasing and funding approach.
- Must include scenarios for build/buy/partner and M&A options.
- Deliverables reviewed by finance, risk/compliance, and HR stakeholders.
