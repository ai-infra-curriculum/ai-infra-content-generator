## Project Narrative

Executives expect the organization to lead industry dialogue on trusted AI infrastructure. You will:

1. Define thought leadership themes tied to strategy, responsible AI, and future infrastructure.
2. Produce flagship whitepaper, standards contribution, and conference presentation.
3. Develop media briefing kit, FAQ, and crisis communication guidance.
4. Engage with industry forums, gather feedback, and document impact metrics.
5. Present influence report and recommendations to executive leadership.

## Deliverables

- Thought leadership package (whitepaper, blog/article drafts, standards proposal).
- Conference presentation deck, speaker notes, demo assets.
- Media/analyst briefing kit (FAQs, talking points, risk mitigation).
- Influence dashboard tracking reach, engagement, and business outcomes.
- Executive report summarizing impact, next steps, and investment asks.
