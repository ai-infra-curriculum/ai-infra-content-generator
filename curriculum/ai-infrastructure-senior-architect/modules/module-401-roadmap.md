# Module Roadmap

> Senior AI Infrastructure Architect | Module 401 modernizes the enterprise AI strategy toolkit (built from legacy Module 401).

## Module Overview

- **Module ID**: MOD-401
- **Module Title**: Enterprise AI Strategy & Vision
- **Target Role(s)**: Senior AI Infrastructure Architect
- **Duration**: 50 hours (16 lecture, 20 lab, 10 portfolio, 4 assessment)
- **Prerequisites**: MOD-301, MOD-309
- **Next Module(s)**: MOD-402, MOD-403, PROJ-501

## Cross-Role Progression

- **Builds On**: Architect-level governance and communication foundations (MOD-301, MOD-309).
- **Adds New Depth**: Board-level strategy, portfolio management, investment modeling.
- **Shared Assets**: Reuses capability maps, ADR templates, and FinOps dashboards from architect role.
- **Differentiators**: Focuses on enterprise-wide transformation, risk, and value realization for AI programs.

## Learning Objectives

- Create 3-5 year AI infrastructure strategy aligned with corporate goals and risk appetite.
- Define capability roadmaps, KPI frameworks, and portfolio management structures.
- Develop business cases, investment scenarios, and stakeholder alignment plans.

## Content Outline

1. **Strategic Analysis** – AI maturity models, value stream mapping, risk/ROI frameworks.
2. **Roadmap Development** – capability planning, milestones, dependencies, investment phasing.
3. **Portfolio Management** – prioritization, governance, OKRs, benefit tracking.
4. **Financial Modeling** – TCO/ROI, sensitivity analysis, budgeting, sustainability metrics.
5. **Stakeholder Alignment** – executive narratives, decision frameworks, change management.
