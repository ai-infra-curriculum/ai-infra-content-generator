# Module Roadmap

> AI Infrastructure Team Lead | Module 808 builds people-first leadership skills (legacy alignment: modules/ai-infrastructure-team-lead/module-808-executive-communication).

## Module Overview

- **Module ID**: MOD-808
- **Module Title**: Executive Communication for Team Leads
- **Target Role(s)**: AI Infrastructure Team Lead
- **Duration**: 42 hours (12 lecture, 14 lab, 8 portfolio, 8 assessment)
- **Prerequisites**: MOD-804
- **Next Module(s)**: MOD-810, PROJ-803

## Cross-Role Progression

- Bridges team lead communications with architect and executive leadership needs.
- Shares narrative frameworks with principal roles to ensure consistency.
- Reduces duplication by reusing storytelling templates.

## Learning Objectives

- Craft concise executive updates and risk briefings.
- Handle Q&A, stakeholder objections, and crisis communications.
- Align delivery narratives with business strategy and customer impact.

## Competency Alignment

| Competency | Proficiency Target | Evidence / Assessment | Role Alignment |
|------------|--------------------|-----------------------|----------------|
| executive-communication | Advanced | Executive update deck & briefing notes | AI Infrastructure Team Lead |
| risk-communication | Advanced | Crisis communication playbook | AI Infrastructure Team Lead |

## Content Outline

1. **Narrative Design** – framing, data storytelling, visual aids.
2. **Executive Presence** – delivery, body language, virtual tactics.
3. **Risk Framing** – scenario planning, escalation messaging.
4. **Q&A Mastery** – objection handling, bridging techniques.
5. **Collaboration** – working with comms/PR/legal teams.

## Hands-On Activities

- Executive update rehearsal with recorded feedback.
- Risk briefing simulation with PR/legal stakeholders.
- Storytelling workshop converting technical metrics to business impact.

## Assessments & Evidence

- Executive update deck presented to leadership panel.
- Crisis communication memo reviewed by comms partner.

## Shared Assets & Legacy Mapping

- Communication templates reused by architect/principal programs.
- Risk briefings integrated into incident command training.
