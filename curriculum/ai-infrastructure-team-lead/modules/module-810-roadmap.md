# Module Roadmap

> AI Infrastructure Team Lead | Module 810 builds people-first leadership skills (legacy alignment: modules/ai-infrastructure-team-lead/module-810-strategic-planning).

## Module Overview

- **Module ID**: MOD-810
- **Module Title**: Strategic Planning & Org Design for Leads
- **Target Role(s)**: AI Infrastructure Team Lead
- **Duration**: 42 hours (12 lecture, 14 lab, 8 portfolio, 8 assessment)
- **Prerequisites**: MOD-806, MOD-808
- **Next Module(s)**: PROJ-803

## Cross-Role Progression

- Bridges team lead planning with architect/principal strategy cycles.
- Shares staffing models and capability roadmaps with HR and finance.
- Avoids duplication by referencing existing strategy templates.

## Learning Objectives

- Craft quarterly plans, OKRs, and capability roadmaps.
- Design staffing plans, hiring profiles, and succession plans.
- Recommend org design or process changes aligned to strategy.

## Competency Alignment

| Competency | Proficiency Target | Evidence / Assessment | Role Alignment |
|------------|--------------------|-----------------------|----------------|
| strategic-planning | Advanced | Quarterly plan & OKR set | AI Infrastructure Team Lead |
| org-design | Advanced | Org proposal & staffing model | AI Infrastructure Team Lead |

## Content Outline

1. **Strategic Alignment** – business goals, platform strategy, OKRs.
2. **Capability Roadmaps** – skills inventory, gaps, investment cases.
3. **Org Design** – structures, squads, guilds, reporting lines.
4. **Financial Alignment** – budgeting, headcount, vendor strategy.
5. **Change Management** – communication, rollout, feedback loops.

## Hands-On Activities

- OKR drafting workshop with executive feedback.
- Org design scenario exercise evaluating trade-offs.
- Budget planning lab aligning resources to roadmap.

## Assessments & Evidence

- Quarterly plan and staffing model presentation.
- Org design proposal reviewed by leadership panel.

## Shared Assets & Legacy Mapping

- Strategic planning templates reused by principal architect teams.
- Staffing models integrated with HR/finance planning cycles.
