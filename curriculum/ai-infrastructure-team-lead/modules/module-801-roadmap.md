# Module Roadmap

> AI Infrastructure Team Lead | Module 801 builds people-first leadership skills (legacy alignment: modules/ai-infrastructure-team-lead/module-801-leadership-fundamentals).

## Module Overview

- **Module ID**: MOD-801
- **Module Title**: Leadership Fundamentals for AI Infrastructure
- **Target Role(s)**: AI Infrastructure Team Lead
- **Duration**: 46 hours (18 lecture, 16 lab, 8 portfolio, 4 assessment)
- **Prerequisites**: MOD-210, MOD-402
- **Next Module(s)**: MOD-802, MOD-805, PROJ-801

## Cross-Role Progression

- Builds on senior technical leadership competencies and introduces people-lead responsibilities.
- Provides leadership artifacts reused by Team Lead, Principal Engineer, and Architect tracks.
- Aligns with human resources partners for promotion calibration consistency.

## Learning Objectives

- Adopt servant leadership principles tailored to AI infrastructure squads.
- Establish decision frameworks, communication cadences, and trust-building practices.
- Create personal leadership charter and stakeholder alignment plan.

| Competency | Proficiency Target | Evidence / Assessment | Role Alignment |
|------------|--------------------|-----------------------|----------------|

## Content Outline

1. **Leadership Mindset** – servant leadership, psychological safety, inclusion.
2. **Decision Frameworks** – RACI, DACI, decision logs, escalation paths.
3. **Communication Channels** – 1:1s, team meetings, async updates.
4. **Stakeholder Alignment** – expectation setting, conflict management.
5. **Personal Operating System** – time management, boundaries, reflection.

## Hands-On Activities

- Leadership charter workshop with peer feedback.
- Difficult conversation role-play with coaching observers.
- Stakeholder mapping lab defining expectations and cadences.

## Assessments & Evidence

- Leadership charter submission with stakeholder buy-in evidence.
- Communication plan review by mentor or director.

## Shared Assets & Legacy Mapping

- Shares leadership artifacts with principal IC tracks for cross-role mentoring.
- Inputs to HR calibration docs to avoid duplication of leadership frameworks.
