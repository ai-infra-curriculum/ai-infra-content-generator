# Module Roadmap

> AI Infrastructure Team Lead | Module 809 builds people-first leadership skills (legacy alignment: modules/ai-infrastructure-team-lead/module-809-risk-incident-management).

## Module Overview

- **Module ID**: MOD-809
- **Module Title**: Risk & Incident Management Leadership
- **Target Role(s)**: AI Infrastructure Team Lead
- **Duration**: 44 hours (12 lecture, 16 lab, 8 project, 8 assessment)
- **Prerequisites**: MOD-802, MOD-807
- **Next Module(s)**: MOD-810, PROJ-803

## Cross-Role Progression

- Combines operations and culture modules to lead resilient incident response.
- Shares incident management artifacts with reliability and security teams.
- Prevents duplication by referencing centralized incident taxonomy.

## Learning Objectives

- Lead cross-functional incident response and coordinate communications.
- Maintain risk registers, mitigation plans, and review cadences.
- Drive blameless postmortems with action tracking and learning loops.

## Competency Alignment

| Competency | Proficiency Target | Evidence / Assessment | Role Alignment |
|------------|--------------------|-----------------------|----------------|
| incident-management | Advanced | Incident response packet & postmortem review | AI Infrastructure Team Lead |

## Content Outline

1. **Risk Frameworks** – identification, scoring, ownership.
2. **Incident Leadership** – roles, comms, escalations.
3. **Postmortem Excellence** – facilitation, follow-up, tooling.
4. **Continuous Learning** – drills, runbooks, KPI tracking.
5. **Stakeholder Reporting** – customer updates, exec reviews.

## Hands-On Activities

- Risk register workshop with simulated scenarios.
- Postmortem facilitation lab with cross-functional observers.
- Incident drill tabletop exercising comms and decision-making.

## Assessments & Evidence

- Risk register submission with mitigation status.
- Postmortem report with documented follow-up actions.

## Shared Assets & Legacy Mapping

- Risk frameworks shared with reliability & security programs.
- Incident artifacts consumed by principal engineer and architect tracks.
