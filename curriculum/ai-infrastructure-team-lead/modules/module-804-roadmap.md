## Module Overview

- **Module ID**: MOD-804
- **Module Title**: Cross-Functional Collaboration & Stakeholder Management
- **Target Role(s)**: AI Infrastructure Team Lead

## Cross-Role Progression

- Aligns team leaders with product and research stakeholder expectations.
- Shares collaboration canvases with architect and principal engineer roles.
- Reduces duplication by centralizing stakeholder mapping templates.

## Learning Objectives

- Facilitate cross-functional planning sessions and conflict resolution.
- Negotiate priorities and resource allocations with partners.
- Maintain stakeholder health metrics and communication plans.

## Content Outline

1. **Stakeholder Discovery** – roles, goals, communication preferences.
2. **Collaboration Frameworks** – RACI variants, service agreements.
3. **Conflict Resolution** – escalation ladders, mediation techniques.
4. **Joint Planning** – OKRs, roadmap sync, dependency management.
5. **Relationship Health** – surveys, feedback loops, alignment checks.

## Hands-On Activities

- Stakeholder mapping exercise with scenario inputs.
- Negotiation role-play balancing competing priorities.
- Collaboration canvas workshop producing shared goals.

## Assessments & Evidence

- Stakeholder engagement plan with feedback cadence.
- Joint roadmap summary reviewed by partner lead.

## Shared Assets & Legacy Mapping

- Collaboration canvases reused by architect and principal engineer teams.
- Alignment dashboards plugged into executive operating rhythms.
