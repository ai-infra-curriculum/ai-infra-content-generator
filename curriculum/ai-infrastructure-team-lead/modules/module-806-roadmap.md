- **Target Role(s)**: AI Infrastructure Team Lead

- Plan and execute roadmaps with dependency mapping and staffing models.
- Run status reviews, risk assessments, and steering communications.
- Implement tooling for progress tracking and transparency.

## Content Outline

1. **Roadmap Planning** – objectives, deliverables, sequencing.
2. **Resourcing** – staffing plans, onboarding, vendor coordination.
3. **Status Management** – dashboards, cadences, visual management.
4. **Risk & Issue Management** – logs, mitigation, escalation.
5. **Tooling** – backlog systems, automation, guardrails.

## Hands-On Activities

- Roadmap planning workshop with dependency modeling.
- Status review simulation with executive stakeholders.
- Risk management lab designing mitigation plans.

## Assessments & Evidence

- Roadmap and staffing plan presentation.
- Status dashboard submission with risk mitigation strategy.

## Shared Assets & Legacy Mapping

- Roadmap templates reused by architect/principal tracks.
- Dashboards integrated into program PMO tooling.
