# Module Roadmap

> AI Infrastructure Team Lead | Module 802 builds people-first leadership skills (legacy alignment: modules/ai-infrastructure-team-lead/module-802-team-operations).

## Module Overview

- **Module ID**: MOD-802
- **Module Title**: Team Operations & Execution Rhythms
- **Target Role(s)**: AI Infrastructure Team Lead
- **Duration**: 46 hours (16 lecture, 18 lab, 8 project, 4 assessment)
- **Prerequisites**: MOD-801, MOD-207
- **Next Module(s)**: MOD-803, MOD-806, MOD-809, PROJ-801

## Cross-Role Progression

- Extends senior SRE/operations practices into leadership accountability.
- Shares incident response templates with principal engineer and architect tracks.
- Supports organizational reliability reviews with consistent runbooks.

## Learning Objectives

- Design on-call rotations, escalation paths, and operational ceremonies.
- Lead incident response and postmortem facilitation with empathy and rigor.
- Implement operational metrics and dashboards for executive reporting.

## Competency Alignment

| Competency | Proficiency Target | Evidence / Assessment | Role Alignment |
|------------|--------------------|-----------------------|----------------|
| team-operations | Advanced | Operational cadence plan & dashboard | AI Infrastructure Team Lead |

## Content Outline

1. **Operational Cadences** – standups, reviews, runbooks, tooling.
2. **On-call Leadership** – rotations, escalation, sustainable pacing.
3. **Incident Command** – roles, communication tree, decision authority.
4. **Postmortems** – facilitation, action tracking, learning culture.
5. **Operational Metrics** – SLIs/SLOs, toil tracking, executive dashboards.

## Hands-On Activities

- Incident simulation tabletop with cross-functional observers.
- Operational rhythm design lab building weekly/monthly cadence.
- Dashboard workshop prioritizing lead-level metrics.

## Assessments & Evidence

- Operational cadence document with stakeholder sign-off.
- Incident response case study with facilitator feedback.

## Shared Assets & Legacy Mapping

- Shares incident playbooks with reliability and security teams.
- Feeds dashboards into principal engineer performance programs.
