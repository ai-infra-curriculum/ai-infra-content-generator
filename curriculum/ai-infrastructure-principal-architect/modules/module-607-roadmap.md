## Module Overview

- **Module ID**: MOD-607
- **Module Title**: Strategic Partnership & Ecosystem Strategy
- **Target Role(s)**: Principal AI Infrastructure Architect
- **Duration**: 40 hours (12 lecture, 16 lab, 8 project, 4 assessment)
- **Prerequisites**: MOD-404, MOD-407
- **Next Module(s)**: PROJ-604, MOD-609

## Cross-Role Progression

- Advances senior partnership work into principal-level governance and commercial orchestration.
- Introduces joint ventures, alliance scorecards, and co-innovation investment models.
- Aligns with corporate development, legal, and procurement stakeholders.

## Learning Objectives

- Develop partnership strategies with hyperscalers, hardware providers, and research ecosystems.
- Negotiate commercial structures, exit criteria, and value-sharing models.
- Set up alliance governance, joint OKRs, and escalation channels.

## Content Outline

1. **Ecosystem Segmentation** – hyperscalers, hardware, academia, startups.
2. **Deal Structures** – revenue sharing, investment models, IP ownership.
3. **Governance** – joint councils, operating rhythms, escalation paths.
4. **Value Measurement** – joint OKRs, shared KPI dashboards, success attribution.
5. **Risk & Compliance** – antitrust, data sharing, regulatory considerations.

- Alliance blueprint workshop with vendor stakeholders.
- Negotiation role-play covering pricing, IP, and success criteria.
- Joint OKR design session aligning internal and partner teams.

## Assessments & Evidence

- Partnership strategy playbook presentation.
- Joint governance charter submission.
