## Module Overview

- **Module ID**: MOD-601
- **Module Title**: Enterprise Architecture Leadership
- **Target Role(s)**: Principal AI Infrastructure Architect
- **Duration**: 50 hours (18 lecture, 20 lab, 8 portfolio, 4 assessment)
- **Prerequisites**: MOD-401, MOD-403
- **Next Module(s)**: MOD-602, MOD-603, PROJ-601

- Builds on Senior Architect strategic roadmaps and governance baselines.
- Adds principal-level funding models, board stewardship, and architecture operating model leadership.
- Shares enterprise architecture templates with the Principal Engineer portfolio.

## Learning Objectives

- Construct enterprise AI architecture playbooks and capability maps with investment guardrails.
- Design global architecture operating models, councils, and decision frameworks.
- Quantify portfolio investments, risk thresholds, and KPIs for executive oversight.
