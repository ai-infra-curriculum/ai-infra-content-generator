## Module Overview

- **Module ID**: MOD-605
- **Module Title**: Responsible AI Programs at Enterprise Scale
- **Target Role(s)**: Principal AI Infrastructure Architect
- **Duration**: 42 hours (14 lecture, 16 lab, 8 project, 4 assessment)
- **Prerequisites**: MOD-405, MOD-408
- **Next Module(s)**: MOD-603, MOD-610, PROJ-602

## Cross-Role Progression

- Extends senior responsible AI frameworks with principal-level governance.
- Adds global audit automation, regulator engagement, and ethics council leadership.
- Aligns with legal, compliance, and security programs through shared controls.

## Learning Objectives

- Stand up enterprise responsible AI councils, policies, and escalation workflows.
- Automate evidence pipelines and audit readiness across AI product lifecycle.
- Coordinate regulator engagement, transparency reporting, and ethics communications.

## Content Outline

1. **RAI Governance** – councils, policy lifecycles, ethical frameworks.
2. **Controls & Automation** – monitoring, documentation, enforcement pipelines.
3. **Regulatory Alignment** – EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, ISO/IEC 42001 mapping.
4. **Transparency & Reporting** – disclosures, scorecards, escalation comms.
5. **Culture & Training** – role-based enablement, change management, ethics training.

## Hands-On Activities

- Controls-as-code lab using sample governance tooling.
- Regulator briefing simulation with legal and public affairs roles.

## Assessments & Evidence

- Responsible AI governance briefing to executive committee.
- Audit readiness checklist with evidence pipeline design.
