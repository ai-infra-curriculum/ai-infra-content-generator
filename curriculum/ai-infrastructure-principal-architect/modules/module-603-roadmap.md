## Module Overview

- **Module ID**: MOD-603
- **Module Title**: Global Governance & Risk Orchestration
- **Target Role(s)**: Principal AI Infrastructure Architect
- **Duration**: 48 hours (16 lecture, 18 lab, 10 project, 4 assessment)
- **Prerequisites**: MOD-405, MOD-406
- **Next Module(s)**: MOD-605, MOD-606, PROJ-602

## Cross-Role Progression

- Builds on senior governance modules with cross-region orchestration and policy automation.
- Introduces sovereign compliance, board risk dashboards, and global assurance programs.
- Aligns with security and compliance specialists through shared control libraries.

## Learning Objectives

- Design global governance operating models with automated policy enforcement.
- Integrate sovereignty, risk, and compliance metrics into executive scorecards.
- Establish assurance cadences, escalation protocols, and audit-ready evidence pipelines.

## Content Outline

1. **Governance Architecture** – councils, charters, decision rights across regions.
2. **Policy Automation** – controls as code, evidence capture, regulatory mapping.
3. **Risk Management** – board heatmaps, appetite statements, escalation flows.
4. **Sovereignty & Compliance** – data residency, cross-border coordination, audit prep.
5. **Assurance & Reporting** – cadences, dashboards, and regulator engagement.

## Hands-On Activities

- Global policy mapping lab with compliance and legal stakeholders.
- Governance tabletop exercise simulating escalation across regions.
- Risk dashboard build using anonymized executive reporting data.

## Assessments & Evidence

- Governance operating model presentation with metrics and RACI.
- Compliance automation playbook with evidence capture strategy.

## Shared Assets & Legacy Mapping

- Extends responsible AI frameworks from MOD-405.
- Shares control libraries with security and compliance programs.
