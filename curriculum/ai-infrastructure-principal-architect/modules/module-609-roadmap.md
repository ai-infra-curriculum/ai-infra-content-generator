## Module Overview

- **Module ID**: MOD-609
- **Module Title**: Executive Communication & Influence at Board Level
- **Target Role(s)**: Principal AI Infrastructure Architect
- **Duration**: 38 hours (12 lecture, 14 lab, 8 portfolio, 4 assessment)
- **Prerequisites**: MOD-402, MOD-409
- **Next Module(s)**: PROJ-603, PROJ-604

## Cross-Role Progression

- Elevates senior executive communication into board, investor, and regulator contexts.
- Introduces crisis leadership, media engagement, and investor relations collaboration.
- Aligns with communications, IR, and public affairs teams for unified messaging.

## Learning Objectives

- Craft board-ready narratives, briefing books, and investment defenses.
- Manage crisis communication playbooks for AI infrastructure incidents.
- Coach executive peers and partner teams on messaging consistency.

3. **Crisis Playbooks** – scenario response, decision escalation, public statements.
4. **Influence Techniques** – negotiation, feedback loops, executive coaching.
5. **Measurement** – sentiment tracking, stakeholder engagement metrics.
