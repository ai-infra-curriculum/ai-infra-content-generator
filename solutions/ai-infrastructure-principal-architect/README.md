- Experience with executive-level stakeholder management

## Distinguishing Principal Architect from Principal Engineer

**Principal Architect (this track):**
- Focus: Strategic vision and organizational alignment
- Scope: Company-wide architecture, multi-year horizons
- Impact: Shapes technology direction for entire organization
- Deliverables: Strategies, roadmaps, frameworks, governance
- Time horizon: 2-5 years
- Stakeholders: Executives, VPs, Board

**Principal Engineer:**
- Focus: Technical depth and execution excellence
- Scope: Hands-on technical leadership, solving hardest problems
- Impact: Raises technical bar, delivers complex systems
- Deliverables: Working systems, technical frameworks, deep expertise
- Time horizon: Quarters to 1-2 years
- Stakeholders: Engineering teams, technical leadership

## Example Strategic Initiatives

- **Multi-Cloud Strategy**: Define 3-year plan for AWS/GCP/Azure adoption
  - Business case: $15M annual savings, risk mitigation
  - Stakeholders: CTO, CFO, VP Engineering

- **ML Platform Consolidation**: Standardize on unified ML platform
  - Impact: Reduce tools from 12 to 3, 40% cost reduction
  - Timeline: 18-month phased migration

- **Infrastructure Modernization**: Cloud-native transformation
  - Scope: Move 500+ services, 200TB data, 5000 GPUs
  - Budget: $30M investment, $50M 3-year savings

- **AI Governance Framework**: Company-wide AI/ML standards
  - Compliance: SOC2, GDPR, model risk management
  - Adoption: 8 business units, 60+ ML teams
