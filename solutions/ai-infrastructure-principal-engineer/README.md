## Distinguishing Principal Engineer from Architect

**Principal Engineer (this track):**
- Focus: Technical depth and execution excellence
- Scope: Hands-on technical leadership, deep expertise
- Impact: Solves the hardest technical problems, raises the bar
- Deliverables: Working systems, performance improvements, technical frameworks

**Principal Architect:**
- Focus: Strategic architecture and organizational alignment
- Scope: Cross-company technical strategy, long-term vision
- Impact: Shapes technology direction for entire organization
- Deliverables: Architecture strategies, roadmaps, organizational standards
