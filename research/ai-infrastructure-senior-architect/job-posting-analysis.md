# Job Posting Analysis — Senior AI Infrastructure Architect

> Sample set: 18 postings (2024–2025) from global enterprises, financial institutions, healthcare, telecom, and hyperscaler advanced research units.

## Summary

- **Regions**: USA (SF Bay, Seattle, NYC), Europe (London, Frankfurt, Zurich), Middle East (Dubai), APAC (Singapore, Sydney).
- **Industries**: Cloud/hyperscaler, healthcare, finance, telecom, autonomous systems, manufacturing.
- **Org Size**: 10k – 200k+ employees; budgets for AI initiatives $50M–$500M.

## Experience Expectations

- **Years of Experience**: 12–18+ years in AI/ML infrastructure, enterprise architecture, or equivalent.
- **Education**: Master’s or PhD preferred; MBA or executive education frequently cited.
- **Certifications**: TOGAF, AWS/GCP/Azure Professional Architect, CISSP/CCSP, FinOps Pro, Responsible AI credentials.

## Insights & Actions

- Curriculum needs heavy emphasis on strategy, executive communication, governance, and innovation leadership beyond technical depth.
- Projects must produce board-level artifacts (roadmaps, strategy decks, risk registers, partnership plans).
- Assessment should include executive simulations, policy/audit reviews, and public thought leadership deliverables.
