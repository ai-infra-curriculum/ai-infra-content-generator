# Job Posting Analysis — AI Infrastructure Team Lead

> Sample set: 22 postings (2024–2025) from hyperscalers, AI-native startups, fintech, healthcare, and autonomous systems organizations.

## Summary

- **Regions**: SF Bay, Seattle, Austin, Boston, London, Dublin, Bangalore, Singapore.  
- **Industries**: Cloud platforms, foundation model startups, financial services, healthcare, autonomous mobility, e-commerce.  
- **Org Size**: 500 – 100k+ employees; teams typically 6–12 engineers with matrixed support from platform and reliability groups.

## Hiring Signals & Requirements

- Most postings require **hands-on technical credibility**: ability to review designs, read code/infra configs, and guide troubleshooting.  
- Strong emphasis on **servant leadership** and **psychological safety**; many mention inclusive leadership or DEI contributions.  
- **Operational excellence** is a differentiator: employers expect leads to drive SLO maturity, incident reviews, and operational dashboards.  
- Preference for candidates with **cross-functional empathy** (product strategy, research experimentation, security/compliance).  
- Several postings include **global coordination** (distributed teams, follow-the-sun on-call) and expect experience managing remote/hybrid squads.
