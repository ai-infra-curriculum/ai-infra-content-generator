## Experience Expectations

- **Years of Experience**: 2-5 years in DevOps/ML infrastructure.
- **Education**: BS in CS/CE/EE or equivalent experience.
- **Preferred Certifications**: CKA/CKAD, AWS/GCP/Azure Associate-level, HashiCorp Terraform Associate.

## Insights & Actions

- Modules should emphasize Kubernetes, multi-cloud operations, and IaC.
- Projects must demonstrate automated pipelines and observability scenarios.
- Assessment should include scenario-based troubleshooting and design reviews.
