## Experience Expectations

- **Years of Experience**: 8-12+ years (senior/architect level), with prior leadership of AI/ML platform initiatives.  
- **Education**: BS required; MS/MBA or equivalent strategic training preferred.  
- **Certifications**: TOGAF, AWS/GCP/Azure Professional Architect, CISSP/CCSP, FinOps Practitioner frequently listed.

## Insights & Actions

- Curriculum must emphasize governance, executive communication, and measurable business impact alongside technical depth.
- Projects should produce architecture artifacts (reference architectures, ADRs, cost models) and stakeholder-ready presentations.
- Assessments should include architecture review boards, executive briefings, and ROI analyses.
