## Experience Expectations

- **Years of Experience**: 0-2 years (entry level). Many postings accept internships or bootcamp graduates.
- **Education**: Bachelor's degree in CS/EE/Data Science or equivalent experience.
- **Preferred Certifications**: Cloud fundamentals (AWS/GCP/Azure), Linux Foundation LFCA, Docker Certified Associate.

## Insights & Actions

- **Curriculum Implication**: Emphasize Python automation, Linux CLI, Docker, and cloud fundamentals early.
- **Gaps**: Introduce monitoring dashboards and runbook writing to mirror job expectations.
- **Next Steps**: Validate skill coverage via module roadmaps and project assignments.
