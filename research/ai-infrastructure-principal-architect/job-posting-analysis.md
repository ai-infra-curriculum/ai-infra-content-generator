## Hiring Signals & Requirements

- Most postings require **15+ years** experience and prior ownership of enterprise AI/ML architecture strategy.  
- Many seek **published thought leadership** (whitepapers, patents, standards participation) and references to **board presentations**.  
- Expect **global travel** (20–40%) and cross-regional leadership of architecture guilds.  
- Alignment with **responsible AI** and **ESG** initiatives increasingly explicit.  
- Several roles require or prefer **MBA/Executive education** plus EA certifications (TOGAF, Open CA).
